About Us

About Joybringers Charity

Joybringers Charity is a youth empowerment organization based in the North East of England, founded in 2024 (Registered Charity No: 1212606). We equip young people up to age 25 with the skills, support, and confidence they need to overcome barriers and build thriving futures.

Our programmes focus on practical learning, personal development, and community connection—helping young people develop real-world skills, boost their well-being, and step confidently into adulthood.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower young people through practical education, creative training, and personal development—enabling them to become confident, skilled contributors to society.

Our Vision

A world where every young person—regardless of background—has access to the tools and support they need to lead a fulfilled, independent life.

How We Make a Difference

Our programmes

Our free programmes help young people gain confidence, life skills, and community connections.

Our key programmes include:

  • Digital Skills Workshops: Coding, game design, website building
  • Creative Arts Training: Dance, music, visual arts
  • Data Aanlysis: Power BI, Excel, DataBases
  • Skills for Life: CV writing, career guidance
  • Well-being & Financial Literacy: Mental health, budgeting
  • Community Trips: Museums, gardens, nature reserves
    • Learn more

How We Measure Success:

  • Youth enrolled in education or training
  • Jobs or internships secured
  • Confidence and well-being improvements
  • Community engagement and leadership

Our Core Values

  • Integrity: Transparent and ethical in all we do.
  • Excellence: Striving for the highest standards.
  • Collaboration: Working with others for greater impact.
  • Empowerment: Unlocking each young person's potential.
  • Sustainability: Creating long-lasting, meaningful change.
