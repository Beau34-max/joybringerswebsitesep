Sucess Stories

Youth Boat Sailing 2025

Date: Sunday, 28th September 2025

Joybringers Charity hosted an unforgettable Youth Boat Cruise for young people aged 14-18. The exclusive cruise was filled with games, friendships, and fun memories.

Spaces filled quickly, showing how eager our young people are to connect and create lasting memories together.

Joybringers Charity New Office

Date: 4th September 2025

Joybringers Charity proudly moved into our new office space at Neon Quorum Business Park, NE12 8BU. This milestone strengthens our capacity to support young people across Newcastle upon Tyne and beyond.

We extend heartfelt thanks to Avire UK for making this possible. Your support helps us create brighter futures.

Blakelaw Family Fun Day - 16th August 2025

Joybringers Charity, with support from Blakelaw Council, hosted a vibrant Family Fun Day at Blakelaw Park. Families enjoyed dancing, barbecue, bouncy castles, and face painting.

The event was filled with joy, connection, and community spirit — a true reflection of our mission to bring happiness. One attendee shared, “Please, bring this joy back to us again!”

Outdoor Fun Day on 31st May 2025 at Carlisle Park

Families and young people enjoyed sack races, football, and swimming. Over 80 people joined in this day of laughter and connection.

These community days foster confidence, friendships, and joy. Thank you to everyone who joined and helped make it special. Donate

Multi-Skilled Workshop - 17th April 2025

The Joybringers Charity hosted a Multi-Skilled Workshop for children and young people in Newcastle upon Tyne. Participants explored web design, coding, dance, and public speaking.

One young attendee shared: “I didn't know I could code or dance in front of people — now I feel like I can do anything!”

This workshop empowered creativity and confidence, helping young people dream big and build practical skills.

Excursion to Alnwick Gardens - 27th December 2024

Joybringers Charity took children and young people on a day trip to Alnwick Gardens. It was filled with fun, laughter, and teamwork.

One attendee said, “I've never seen anything like this before. It was so much fun, and I learnt so much about plants and teamwork.”

We're committed to enriching experiences that inspire and empower young people.

Scratch Coding Workshops - August 2024

Dates: 22nd & 31st August 2024

Engaging online and in-person workshops teaching young people the basics of coding using Scratch. Participants created their own games and animations.

“By the end of the session, I could build my own simple animation!”

CV Clinic and Career Talk - July & May 2024

Our Free CV Clinic and Mock Interview Workshop featured expert speakers who shared interview and CV tips. It boosted participant confidence for job readiness.

Followed by our Online Career Talk on 25th May 2024 — helping young people prepare for the future.
