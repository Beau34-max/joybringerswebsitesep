Cookies Policy

Last updated: February 21, 2024

This Cookies Policy explains what Cookies are and how We use them. You should read this policy so You can understand what type of cookies We use, or the information We collect using Cookies and how that information is used.

Cookies do not typically contain any information that personally identifies a user, but personal information that we store about You may be linked to the information stored in and obtained from Cookies. For further information, see our Privacy Policy.

The Use of Cookies

Types of Cookies We Use

Session Cookies - Deleted when you close your browser.

Persistent Cookies - Remain on your device until deleted.

Necessary / Essential Cookies

Type: Session Cookies
Purpose: These are essential to provide you with services and enable website features like logging in or forms. They prevent fraudulent use of user accounts.

Functionality Cookies

Type: Persistent Cookies
Purpose: These remember your preferences, such as login or language settings, to give you a better experience.
