Volunteer Opportunities

Accountant Volunteer

Assist with bookkeeping, accounts management, and financial reporting for the charity.

Secretary Volunteer

Support board and staff meetings, take minutes, manage correspondence, and handle admin tasks.

Compliance Manager Volunteer

Ensure policies, safeguarding, and governance standards are followed across our activities.

Digital Media Analyst Volunteer

Monitor and report on digital media performance, tracking engagement and improving strategy.

Project Manager

Oversee charity projects, manage deadlines, assign responsibilities, and ensure successful delivery.

Food Bank Volunteer

Support in collecting surplus food donations from partner stores like ASDA, KFC, Tesco and Nandos.

Business Analyst

Analyze data and trends, provide actionable insights, and guide business decision-making.

Website Designer/Manager

Design and update Joybringers' website, ensuring accessibility, functionality, and appeal.

Fundraising Coordinator

Plan and coordinate fundraising campaigns to raise awareness and resources for Joybringers.

IT Workshop Assistant

Support delivery of IT skills workshops, helping young people learn coding and digital skills.

Community Outreach Volunteer

Engage with communities, build connections, and represent Joybringers at events and activities.

Counselling Support Manager

Provide emotional support and coordinate counselling sessions for children and young people.

Marketing Manager

Develop and implement strategies to promote Joybringers, manage campaigns and social media.

Graphic Designer

Create appealing designs for marketing, campaigns, and promotional materials.

Parenting Workshop Facilitator

Lead workshops for parents on child development, positive parenting, and family well-being.

Language Tutor

Support children in improving reading, writing, and communication skills.

Data and Research Analyst

Assist in data collection, analysis, and reporting to measure program impact and success.

Volunteer Recruitment Specialist

Lead recruitment efforts, onboard new volunteers, and ensure smooth integration into the team.
